Bookmarks Demo


Use this demo to experience the capabilities of the bookmarks API.

The report's existing bookmarks are fetched and displayed on the left menu, you can switch between available bookmarks by
clicking on the desired bookmark's name.

Change the report state by applying filters, using the filter pane on the right, or by clicking on a visual and applying cross-highlight.
To capture the new report state click 'Capture Bookmark' button, the new bookmark will appear on the left.

In order to experience bookmark share capability, select the bookmark you would like to share and click on the share icon which will appear
right next to the bookmark's name, copy the given URL to a new tab and you will see the embedded report with the wanted bookmark.
